Python Software Engineers @Online Company (Ireland)

What is the Opportunity?

Have you ever considered relocation to another country? Maybe to global leader in IT? Then maybe you should consider Ireland. Ireland is home to major multi-nationals, established scaling Irish companies and cool start-ups. Pieced together with a rich history, a diverse and vibrant workforce and attractive salaries, then maybe Ireland is the next place for you...

We are seeking numerous Pythonists & Pythonistas for numerous Junior and Senior Software Engineering positions.

Any detail on the company?

One of our clients we are hiring for in Dublin, is a leader in their industry. They have created their own in-house product and technology which has 100,000s of concurrent customers.

What can I expect in this job?

You will be working as part of a very established and well knit Software Engineering teams of passionate Python programmers. These people work together effectively, are eager to collaborate and help each other succeed in both developing the company’s platform and their own careers and knowledge.

What do I need to bring?

  • We are considering people with 1 / 3 / 5+ software engineering experience
  • A degree in Computer Science (or equivalent IT degree) is preferred
  • Expertise and passion for all things Python is needed
  • If you have dabbled with Django or Flask or something else that would be nice
  • Asynchronous programming experience with tools like Twisted or Tornado is advantageous
  • A reasonable level of English is good too. Conversational English at a minimum.

What can I earn?

Salaries vary depending on level of experience but from €35 – 65K+ plus some nice benefits on top.

What do I need to do to get this job?

All you need to do is give Gema Garcia a call on +353 89 242 4254 or drop an email to gema.garcia@e-frontiers.com to have a chat. No worries if you do not have a CV give me a call anyway to discuss.
